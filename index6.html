The Plan

Why Colorado?

"

Nathan is turning another year older, and we're heading to the Rockies to celebrate him right. We've locked down a private cabin at Mount Princeton Hot Springs Resort in Buena Vista as our home base. The cabin features a full kitchen, sleeps up to 8 across a King, two Queens, and a sleeper sofa, and includes property-wide hot springs access. For this version, shared costs are estimated using a 6-person split.

There is room to keep the trip flexible, too. If you want to squeeze in something extra, Mount Princeton has plenty to explore, and anyone who prefers a more traditional hotel setup can look into booking one of the resort's lodge rooms instead of joining the cabin split.
