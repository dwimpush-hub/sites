Mount Princeton Hot Springs Resort

Geothermal pools, Collegiate Peaks views, and a cabin with everyone under one roof.

Perched at 8,900 feet in the Collegiate Peaks Wilderness, Mount Princeton Hot Springs Resort sits along Chalk Creek with natural geothermal water flowing into a sprawling system of pools, slides, and creekside soaking channels. The private cabin sleeps the full group and comes with property-wide hot springs access — the perfect home base for a mountain adventure.

  • Private cabin with full kitchen — King, two Queens, and sleeper sofa
  • Full access to geothermal hot springs pools, slides, and creekside channels
  • Dedicated Spa & Club for adults (16+)
  • Complementary mountain bike and snowshoe rentals on property
  • Minutes from downtown Buena Vista dining and trailheads
  • Surrounded by 14,000-foot Collegiate Peaks — one of the most scenic settings in Colorado

The Numbers — Full Day

14 miles. Near-continuous Class IV rapids. One of the hardest commercially run rivers in America.

The Numbers section of the Arkansas River earns its reputation — numbered rapids one through five-plus drop through a tight canyon with almost no rest in between. It's a serious day on the water and one the birthday crew won't forget. Full wetsuit and splash jacket package included, riverside BBQ lunch midway through.

  • 14+ miles of near-continuous Class III–IV whitewater on the Arkansas River
  • Riverside BBQ lunch included mid-river
  • Wetsuit and splash jacket package included
  • Expert guides navigate every technical drop
  • Ages 15+ required for this advanced section

Royal Gorge Route Railroad

Glass-topped dome cars winding through 1,000-foot granite walls.

The Royal Gorge Route Railroad is one of America's most spectacular scenic train rides — vintage Vista Dome cars with glass ceilings roll through the heart of the Royal Gorge, a canyon carved over millions of years with walls towering 1,000 feet above the river below. A fresh grill menu is served onboard, and an open-air gondola car puts you completely in the elements.

  • Glass-ceiling Vista Dome cars with panoramic canyon views above and around
  • Open-air gondola car for the full canyon immersion experience
  • Fresh grill menu prepared and served onboard
  • ~90 minutes winding through one of Colorado's most iconic natural wonders
  • Departs Cañon City — about 1h 15min from Buena Vista
Explore Royal Gorge Route Railroad →

Browns Canyon Half Day

Colorado's most beloved whitewater stretch — and the perfect family adventure.

Browns Canyon National Monument is considered one of the best whitewater rafting sections in all of Colorado. The half-day trip delivers the perfect balance: exciting Class II–III rapids mixed with calmer stretches where you can soak in the stunning high-desert canyon scenery. Guides share local geology, history, and plenty of river stories along the way. First-timers and experienced rafters alike leave grinning.

  • Half-day trip — 1:30 PM to ~4:30 PM Sunday, August 2
  • Class II–III rapids — fun and exciting for everyone, approachable for first-timers
  • Browns Canyon National Monument — some of Colorado's most beautiful canyon scenery
  • Named rapids: Zoom Flume, Big Drop, The Toilet Bowl — each with its own story
  • Guides share local history, geology, and river lore throughout
  • Check-in 45 minutes before launch — arrive by 12:45 PM at the Buena Vista Outpost
  • Ages 7+ welcome — this one's great for the whole family!
